Arcane Glass Mobile Game Project
Download the production log

Arcane Glass is an original mobile game which I am in the process of developing. Originally, it was created as a University project with a team of fellow students but it was not completed due to the ambitious size of the project and the short time frame given in which to complete it. I have taken it upon myself to remake this project and this is enabling me to use the experience to grow and hone my skills in all aspects of game development. Currently, it is in a pre-alpha state; many of the mechanics are yet to be added and the visual elements are just placeholders. Further developments will be uploaded here and I hope you will join me on the journey.

Uncharted Inspired Game

This project was developed for University and advanced my skills in level design, modelling and sculpting, animation, and programming. The task was to develop an original concept inspired by Uncharted, the Naughty Dog series of adventure games. I created a exploration of an island once occupied by Roman forces, now held by mercenaries in search of ancient treasure. A cutscene led the player through the events leading up to landing on the beach of the island, including an ambush by the mercenaries and a near-death experience for the protagonist.
